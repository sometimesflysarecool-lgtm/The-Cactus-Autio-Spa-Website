Why Ceramic Coating

More than a wax — it's a bond

Unlike wax or sealants that sit on top of your paint, ceramic coating chemically bonds to the clear coat — creating a semi-permanent layer of protection that doesn't wash away.

In Arizona's brutal heat and UV environment, this matters more than anywhere else. Your paint fades, oxidizes, and degrades faster here. Ceramic coating is the most effective long-term defense available.

Chemically bonds to paint

Not a topcoat — a permanent layer that becomes part of your clear coat.

Water & dirt bead off

Hydrophobic surface repels water, mud, bird droppings, and environmental fallout.

Lasts years, not weeks

A properly applied coating lasts 1–5 years with basic maintenance.

FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before booking.
How long does ceramic coating last?
With proper prep and maintenance, a professional-grade ceramic coating typically lasts 1–5 years. Factors like wash habits, parking conditions, and Arizona sun exposure all play a role.
Does my car need paint correction before coating?
We include a light polishing step with every coating service. If your paint has deeper scratches or heavy swirl marks, we'll discuss a full paint correction pass beforehand — ceramic coating seals in whatever is underneath, so prep matters.
Can you apply ceramic coating at my home?
Yes — we're fully mobile. We'll come to your home in Mesa or Gilbert with everything needed. We just need a shaded area or garage to work in for optimal curing conditions.
How do I get a price?
Pricing varies by vehicle size, paint condition, and coating tier. Reach out to us directly and we'll give you a custom quote based on your specific vehicle.
