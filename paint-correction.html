Good To Know

What a One-Step Polish Does Not Do

A one-step polish is a paint enhancement service, not a full paint correction. It will NOT remove deep scratches, heavy swirl marks, water spot etching, or other significant paint defects. For those issues, a multi-step correction would be required. We believe in being upfront so you know exactly what results to expect.
