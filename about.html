Meet the Owner

Andrew Okonoski

Founder & Lead Detailer · The Cactus Auto Spa

I started The Cactus Auto Spa because I genuinely love what a proper detail can do for a car and because I was tired of seeing people settle for less. Based right here in Mesa and Gilbert, I built this business from the ground up with one rule: treat every vehicle like it's my own.

With hands-on experience, I've worked on everything from daily drivers to exotics. Every job I take, I show up myself, no subcontractors, no substitutes. When you book with The Cactus Auto Spa, you're getting me, my products, and my full attention from start to finish.

This isn't a franchise. It's a local small business, and my reputation in this community matters more to me than any single job. That's why I don't cut corners, not on product quality, not on time, not on the details that most people don't even notice but make all the difference.

Why We're Different

The Cactus Difference

Five things that set us apart from every other detailing option in the East Valley.

You Get the Owner, Every Time

There's no crew, no rotation, no hired hand who just started last week. Every job is done by me personally — which means you get the same level of care and consistency on your fifth detail as you did on your first.

White-Glove From Booking to Finish

The experience starts before I ever touch your car. Easy booking, clear communication, and a walkthrough when I'm done so you know exactly what was done and why. No guesswork, no surprises.

Local to the East Valley

I live and work in the same community you do. Mesa and Gilbert aren't just service areas — they're home. That means I care about my reputation here in a way a national franchise never could.

Premium Products, No Shortcuts

I use professional-grade products on every vehicle — not whatever's cheapest. Paint correction compounds, ceramic coatings, interior protectants — every product is chosen because it's the best option for your car's finish.

Mobile — We Come to You

No dropping your car off. No waiting rooms. I come to your home. Your time matters, and I've built this service around that from day one.
